3.18 Final

Using only your paper, pen/pencil, and your brain.

Write a paragraph (topic, two sentences, conclusion) that discusses one thing you learned or remembered from each day of class and from each assignment.

There are 42 classes and 17 assignments. You’ll get 0.0300 credit for every day you did a good job on. This means you can get about 0.7700 of points of extra credit. Since this assignment is worth 0.2000 of your grade, that means each day is worth 0.0060 of your grade and you have the opportunity to increase your grade by 0.1540 by doing this extra credit.

You should anticipate almost filling up a "blue book" if you don’t do extra credit. That’s about 5-ish pages (front and back) on normal lined paper. Obviously I don’t care about the page length, but that’s a rough idea of the level of detail I’m expecting.

Please have the paragraphs in class-order in the book. (Whether you write them in class-order is up to you.) Please make the class number very obvious for each paragraph.

Include your NetID on the first page.

After you turn it in, I will grade it on the site. I will automatically do your self-eval for you. You can go to the site later and see my comments and your final grade for the class.

If you would like to have your book returned to you, write "KEEP" next your NetID. I will keep it in my office and you can come pick it up later.

Although finals are only normally three hours, I am willing to stay in this room until we are kicked out or it is 4pm.
