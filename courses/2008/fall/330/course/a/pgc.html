Garbage Collection (Presented)

Choose one of the following options:

  1. Ask a question about garbage collection and find its answer.
  2. Find out about how some system does its garbage collection; say something interesting about it.
  3. Think about a way to make garbage collection better.
  4. Think about a reason why garbage collection doesn't work well in some application.

Be prepared to give a 5 minute presentation in class about your inquiry.
